SUMAN TRIPATHY

I'm a sophomore at UC Berkeley majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science as well as Cognitive Science.

About Me

I was born and raised in Nor Cal and even decided to stay here for college. After I get my degrees, I hope to travel, join Teach for America, and explore my passions.

Outside of school, I love making song mashups, playing volleyball, and rewatching episodes of The Office while drinking coffee. Art, nature, and music intrigue me because I consider them the most essential sources to my humanity.

I FOLLOW MY PASSIONS

Through a double major in EECS and Cognitive Science, I hope to gain both the technical and rational skills needed to study Machine Learning, Artificial Intellifence, and Brain-Machine Interfaces.

I'm awed by technology today but fear that one day, it may in fact destroy humanity. Therefore, I wanted to study the interconnection and dependency between the technology and the brain to create a protected but powerful relationship.

As a second year, I've only done some general projects and courses, but I am soon to take more specialized classes like "Intro to Artificial Intelligence." I also hope to intern at a Machine Learning-focused company this summer as well."
